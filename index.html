What Is Adiwahana

AdiWahana is a micro, small, and medium enterprise specializing in Balinese handmade crafts. We offer a variety of wooden art, sculptures, and Bali souvenirs, blending traditional craftsmanship with a modern touch. We support local artisans and promote sustainable tourism in Bali.
